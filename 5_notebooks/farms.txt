Farm Size Distribution — Egypt

## Using Su et al. (2022) gridded farm-size-specific dataset and Egyptian agricultural census data:

Dataset: Su et al. (2022) — ESSD, Zenodo DOI: 10.5281/zenodo.6976249

What the dataset contains:

Global raster at 5 arcmin resolution (~9.2 km per cell)
11 farm size classes (0–1, 1–2, 2–5, 5–10, 10–20, 20–50, 50–100, 100–200, 200–500, 500–1000, >1000 ha)
27 crops (GAEZv4 version) or 42 crops (SPAM2010 version)
2 or 4 farming systems (irrigated / rainfed)
Based on Ricciardi et al. (2018) agricultural census compilation for 56 countries
For Egypt specifically:

Egypt is NOT in the 56 countries — absent from Ricciardi et al. (2018) due to lack of publicly available standardised agricultural census data
Dataset cannot be used directly for Egypt
